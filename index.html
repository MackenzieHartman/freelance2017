About Us

At Great Heights, we are committed to to the success of each and every child.
Our comphrehensive, personalized approach focuses on working together with families and caregivers to enure we efficiently identify challenges and provide the most effective treatment possible. We're committed to helping families find hope and achieve greatness. We know how challenging and difficult it can feel at times and we make a promise to partner with you throughout the entire journey.

Dedication

We strive to help children succeed in every setting, addressing the specific needs of every child to them up for success.

Progress

Teachers can expect to progressively see positive changes in their students who receive treatment at Great Heights.

Compassion

Great Heights is a place for children to feel celebrated and loved through compassionate care and treatment.
