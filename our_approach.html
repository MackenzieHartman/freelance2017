Dedication


At Great Heights, we are committed to the success of each and every child. Our comprehensive, personalized approach focuses on working together with families and caregivers to ensure we efficiently identify challenges and provide the most effective treatment possible. We’re committed to helping families find hope and achieve greatness. We know how challenging and difficult it can feel at times and we make a promise to partner with you throughout the entire journey.

Progress


Progress happens rooted in a proven approach. Applied behavioral analysis is a scientific discipline that focuses on the application of learning principles to change behavior. At Great Heights, we start by getting to know your child so we can understand unique challenges and help shape constructive behaviors.

Compassion


Our therapists have first-hand experience with the struggles that families with ASD children experience. We understand the feelings of despair, frustration, sadness and embarrassment that so many parents feel when caring for a child with autism. We see care and love through the tears and pain of parents and children. We commit to working with you to build a brighter future for your child and your family.


Supporting Statements/Evidence:

Throughout the course of treatment our clients become family. We know that by working together with parents and caregivers we can better serve their child.

We truly care about the success of every child which is why we make building relationships with families a priority.

Receiving an ASD diagnosis can be scary and knowing where to begin overwhelming. Through every step, we are a shoulder to lean on and a listening ear for our families.
